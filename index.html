About Fish Fury

After taking over the rest of the world, the birds are heading towards one of the last piranha colonies. You are the only one who stands in their way!

Stop the bird invasion: eat 'em, ram them, slice 'em, whatever it takes to show 'em your 'Fish Fury'!

Game Highlights

  • Easy intuitive game play, only swiping your finger
  • Physics engine
  • 5 different birds
  • Cool graphics and animations
  • Game center support
